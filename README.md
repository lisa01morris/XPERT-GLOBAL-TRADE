Xpert Global Trade – Fintech Dashboard

A powerful and fully-featured fintech dashboard prototype designed to manage accounts, payments, and transactions efficiently. Ideal for financial platforms, banking apps, or trading dashboards.

Features
	•	Money Transfers: Send funds via multiple secure methods, including peer-to-peer transfers.
	•	Deposit Methods: Support for various deposit options.
	•	Account Management: Suspend, block, or place accounts on hold for security purposes.
	•	Loan Applications: Submit and track loan requests seamlessly.
	•	Donations: Enable charity donations and contributions.
	•	Card Management: Activate cards and open new accounts with applicable fees.
	•	Benefits & Rewards: Track user benefits and reward programs.
	•	Bill Payments: Pay utilities, subscriptions, and services directly from the dashboard.
	•	Tax Management: Monitor tax-related actions and refunds.
	•	Credit Monitoring: View and manage account credit score and financial health.
	•	Analytics & Reports: Visualize and track transactions, account activity, and performance metrics.
